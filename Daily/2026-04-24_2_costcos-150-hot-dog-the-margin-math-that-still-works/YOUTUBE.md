## DESCRIPTION (100 words)

Costco has sold a hot dog and soda combo for $1.50 since 1985. In that same period, US inflation rose 185%. Most people credit founder Jim Sinegal's stubbornness — but the real story is in a California factory, a Nebraska chicken plant, and a single line from Costco's December 2022 earnings call. CFO Richard Galanti told analysts the food court runs as a "member benefit," not a profit center. What looks like sentiment is a supply chain system. This video breaks down the margin math, the vertical integration playbook, and why the $1.50 price is the cheapest billboard Costco ever built.
