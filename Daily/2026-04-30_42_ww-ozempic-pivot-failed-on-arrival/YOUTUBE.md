## Description (100 words + mandatory trademark block)

In April 2023, WeightWatchers paid $106 million for Sequence, a GLP-1 telehealth platform, and declared itself a clinical weight-loss company. Fourteen months later, it filed Chapter 11. The mainstream narrative blames Ozempic. The SEC filings tell a different story: more than $1.4 billion in long-term debt that predated the GLP-1 era entirely. This is the case study of a company that had 22 months to act, the wrong capital structure to respond, and a brand identity that made clinical credibility structurally impossible. Sources: WW 8-K (April 2023), 10-K FY2022, SDNY Case 24-11988.
